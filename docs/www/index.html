The WWW Story

Our Wine Wednesdays are a way for people to come together in the spirit of inclusivity, creativity, and community. They change location every week in NYC as we rely on hosts to offer their home to our community. What started over 7 years ago as a way for summer interns to connect in San Francisco has developed into a national community founded on a few core values:

No work talk -

we are here to connect on a human/creative level

Everyone contributes -

bring a bottle of wine, cheese, cake, juice, or something for sharing

Be excellent to everyone -

zero-tolerence policy for any form of harassment
